MyOhio.gov

MyOhio.gov is used by State of Ohio employees to manage Payroll and Base Benefits. MyOhio.gov is designed to allow State of Ohio employees to safely and securely manage certain portions of their personal information. Employees are able to view and print payroll and compensation information, including their leave balances.

Employee Guidebook

Dear New Employee:

The Members and staff of the Ohio House of Representatives welcome you in the opportunity to serve the citizens of the State of Ohio with you while at the same time upholding the integrity and tradition of this legislature.

The House would like to make sure you are well informed of the many services and benefits it has to offer in making your employment as rewarding as possible. To assist in this regard an Administrative Reference Guide has been assembled for your use. This guide discusses various topics of interest to you from health care benefits to the House postage policy.

Please take the time to review the information in the Administrative Reference Guide. If you have any questions, please call the House Administrative Office at 614-466-4308.

Conference Rooms
